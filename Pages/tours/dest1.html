About the Journey

Explore the cultural heart of Japan with our curated tour of Kyoto’s temples, zen gardens, and historic neighborhoods. From ancient shrines to bamboo forests, this journey immerses you in the traditions and beauty of Japan.

Tour Highlights

  • ⛩ Visit the iconic Fushimi Inari Shrine with its thousands of torii gates
  • 🏯 Discover the beauty of Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion)
  • 🎎 Walk through Kyoto’s historic Gion geisha district
  • 🍵 Experience a traditional Japanese tea ceremony
  • 🌿 Explore the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

Tour Details

Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights

Price: ₹3,50,000 per person

Includes: Accommodation, guided tours, entrance tickets, and selected meals.
